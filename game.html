Memory Monsters

Start Playing

You need to choose a card and click on it to turn it over and discover the monster's picture behind it. Then select another card and turn it over as well (click on it). If the two cards are a matching pair (The same monster's picture appears), Then those cards will remain flipped. If it's not a match, it will flip back over. The game continues in this fashion until all the cards are played.

Pay attention- Once you click on the first card a clock starts, and stops when you make your final match. You can't see the time until you finish the game, then you will be notified about your result.

Winning the Game

You can win the game once all the cards have been flipped and matched.
